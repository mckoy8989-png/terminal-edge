Straight answers to the questions every client asks before we start building.

Every price listed covers the full scope described in that tier — design, development, testing, deployment, and the specified support window. There are zero hidden costs. You'll receive a detailed scope document before we start, and the final price is locked in unless you request scope changes (which we'll always quote separately before proceeding).
Fixed-price for defined scopes — always. You know the total cost before we write a single line of code. For Enterprise and custom projects, I'll provide a detailed estimate after our discovery call. I don't do hourly billing because it misaligns incentives; you should never pay more just because something took longer.
Bank transfer (ACH/wire), credit/debit cards via Stripe, PayPal, and cryptocurrency (BTC/ETH/USDC). Invoices are sent via email with a secure payment link. For international clients, I accommodate your preferred method whenever possible.
Yes. The standard split is 50% upfront to begin work and 50% on delivery. For larger Enterprise projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments — for example, 30% at kickoff, 30% at mid-build review, and 40% on final delivery. We'll agree on the schedule before signing off.
Scope changes happen — and that's okay. If you need additional features or pages beyond the original agreement, I'll provide a clear, itemized quote for the extra work before starting. No surprises, no retroactive billing. Small tweaks within reason are usually absorbed; anything significant gets documented and approved by you first.
