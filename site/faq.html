General
Terminal Edge is a solo-operated digital engineering studio specializing in custom websites, AI-powered workflows, trading bots, and bespoke automation solutions. We combine deep technical expertise with a lean, no-overhead structure to deliver enterprise-quality results at a fraction of the cost of traditional agencies.
Terminal Edge is run by a single senior engineer with years of experience across full-stack development, machine learning, and financial technology. Every line of code, every architecture decision, and every deployment is handled directly — no hand-offs, no miscommunication, no junior devs learning on your dime.
We take on a wide range of digital engineering projects — from marketing websites and SaaS platforms to AI chatbots, data pipelines, algorithmic trading systems, and custom browser extensions. If it involves code and solves a real problem, we're interested. We're especially drawn to complex, high-impact builds that other shops shy away from.
Agencies layer in project managers, junior developers, and overhead costs that inflate your bill without improving the product. With Terminal Edge, you work directly with the person writing your code — which means faster turnarounds, clearer communication, and a level of craft that assembly-line agencies can't replicate. Think boutique watchmaker versus factory floor.

Pricing
Projects typically range from $2,000 for focused single-feature builds to $25,000+ for full-scale platforms. Every project receives a detailed scope document and fixed quote before work begins — no surprise invoices, no scope-creep billing. Visit our pricing page for tier breakdowns, or reach out for a custom estimate tailored to your exact needs.
We strongly prefer fixed-price contracts because they align incentives — you know exactly what you'll pay, and we're motivated to build efficiently. For ongoing retainers or advisory work where scope is fluid, we offer hourly or monthly arrangements. The billing model is always agreed upon before a single line of code is written.
We accept bank transfers (ACH/wire), PayPal, Stripe (credit/debit cards), and select cryptocurrency payments (BTC, ETH, USDC). International clients can pay via Wise for low-fee cross-border transfers. Invoices are sent via a professional billing platform with clear line items and payment tracking.
Yes. Our standard payment structure is milestone-based: 40% upfront to begin development, 30% at the midpoint review, and 30% upon final delivery and approval. For larger projects, we can customize the payment schedule to fit your cash-flow needs. Every milestone is clearly defined so you always know what you're paying for.
Scope changes happen — we get it. Small adjustments within the spirit of the original brief are absorbed at no extra charge. For significant pivots or feature additions, we issue a transparent change order with updated timelines and costs before any new work begins. You'll never be surprised by a bigger-than-expected invoice.
We offer a satisfaction guarantee on all milestone deliverables — if a milestone doesn't meet the agreed-upon specifications, we'll revise it until it does. If you choose to cancel before a milestone is delivered, any unearned portion of the payment is refunded. Our goal is for you to feel confident at every stage of the project.
Support
Every project includes a 30-day post-launch support window at no extra charge, covering bug fixes, minor tweaks, and deployment assistance. After that, you can opt into a monthly maintenance retainer or simply reach out on an as-needed basis. We also provide a complete handoff package — documentation, architecture diagrams, and a walkthrough call — so your team can confidently take the reins.
Timelines depend on complexity: a focused landing page can ship in 3–5 days, a full web application in 3–6 weeks, and a complex platform with AI integrations in 6–12 weeks. We provide a realistic timeline in the proposal — not an optimistic fantasy — and we hit our deadlines. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee when deadlines are non-negotiable.
You'll receive regular progress updates via your preferred channel — Slack, Discord, email, or a shared project board (Notion/Linear). We send weekly summary reports with completed tasks, upcoming milestones, and any blockers. For real-time discussions, we schedule focused calls rather than endless meetings. Your time is respected as much as ours.
